The Best of Japan's Stationery Awards 2025. Since 2013, stationery experts from popular Japanese retailers like LOFT, TSUTAYA, and HANDS have gathered together to judge newly released stationery and give awards to the best products every year. For the 2025 Japan Stationery Awards, over a thousand items were submitted by various manufacturers and rated based on their function, design, and creativity. The results are published in Bunbōguyasan Taishō (文房具屋さん大賞) by Fushosha Publishing Co. This magazine is often referred to as the “bible” for stationery fans.
